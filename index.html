About Us

Who We Are

Negilayogi samaja seva trust (R) is a social service organization formed during 1997 with few people who were interested to help the rural children of agriculturists. Dr K V Puttappa (Kuvempu) has coined this word Negilayogi in his literary writing which refers to the farmers. Basically most of the farmers are living in the rural area and majority of them belong to vokkaliga community in the southern Karnataka region. The main aim of the Negilayogi samaja seva trust is to organize the people of this community to achieve social, economic growth and to establish harmony within and outside the community. Besides, the trust helps the children of negilayogi in providing proper education, training for competitive exam, career guidance personality development course etc, so that students get viable jobs.

Activity

  • Creation of Professionals groups for social service

    Different groups of professionals who are residing in Mysore city and district have been created to take up social and cultural services to our community. Mainly such groups are: Doctors group, Engineers and technocrats group, Advocates group, Educators group, Business and service sector groups. Detailed activities of these groups may be seen under menu professionals groups.

  • Training for competitive examinations

    Well equipped training center has been set up to train the rural students for all competitive examinations. Well equipped library with computers and internet facility has been provided. Dedicated teachers train the students for different competitive examinations . Training is conducted for all government posts starting from SDC post to KAS and allied examination.

  • Matrimonial service

    Trust has started matrimonial services for vokkaliga community(including sub castes) brides and bridegrooms with formal registration in the trust office. Parents can notify their daughter/son details with photos in the center and the files are maintained for bride and bridegrooms separately. Further brides and bridegrooms are classified according to their qualification, employment etc. Consultancy centre is kept open from 11 am to 5 pm on all days for visitors except Tuesday being holiday.

  • Negilabhagya monthly News Paper

    Trust is publishing every month a news paper called “Negilabhagya’’ and distributes to its members. It is 16 pages monthly paper reaches to subscriber by post through out Karnataka. Subscription fee is Rs.500 for 5 years.
